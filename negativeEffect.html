Negative Impact of Immigration in Australia

  • A relative increase in the scarcity of resources and natural assets
  • Infrastructure to become overburdened, and requires expensive new infrastructure to be built
  • Wages of workers to fall
  • Unemployment
  • A shortage of affordable housing
  • Environmental degradation
